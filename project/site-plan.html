FlavorFusion - Site Plan

Site Name

FlavorFusion

FlavorFusion was chosen to reflect the blend of global food cultures and flavors. It's simple, memorable, and highlights the site's focus on diverse, tasty and easy-to-make recipes.

Site Purpose

FlavorFusion helps users find easy, affordable recipes by offering a searchable recipe database, cultural food blogs, and budget-friendly cooking tips. The site aims to make home cooking fun, flavorful, and accessible for everyone.

Scenarios

  • What's a quick, healthy meal I can cook with ingredients I already have?
  • How can I find affordable recipe that fit my keto diet?
  • Where can I learn about the cultural background of a popular dish like Jollof Rice?

Color Schema

  • Tomato Red (#E94B3C): Used for Buttons, recipe titles, and important highlights. This color evokes appetite and draws attention.
  • Olive Green (#6B8E23): Used for Navigation bar, accents, and category tags. It gives a natural, earthy feel associated with fresh ingredients.
  • Light Cream (#FFF8E7): Used for Main background to keep the site light, warm, and easy to read.
  • Charcoal Gray (#333333): Used for Body text to ensure high readability on light backgrounds.

Typography

The site will use the following fonts:

  • Poppins (sans-serif) for headings
  • Open Sans (sans-serif) for body text
  • Dancing Script (cursive - optional) for body text
